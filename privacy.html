Welcome to KeyMistry (“we”, “our”, “us”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website https://bumblebee-3.github.io/KeyMistry/.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to improve your experience and website performance:

Local storage data:
We may store small pieces of data in your browser’s localStorage (e.g., your app settings, preferences, or progress).
This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any external servers.

Usage analytics:
We use tools such as Web Insights to collect anonymous data about how visitors use the site — for example, pages visited, time spent, or general device information.
This helps us understand usage trends and improve functionality.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as names, emails, or passwords) unless explicitly provided through future features (like sign-ups or contact forms).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve the app experience and performance
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Debug or enhance new features

We never sell or rent your data to anyone.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

KeyMistry does not use traditional cookies.
Instead, we use localStorage, which stores data directly in your browser and can be cleared manually by you anytime via your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics tools (like Web Insights) that collect anonymous usage statistics.
These tools have their own privacy policies and do not access personal data stored locally on your device.

5. Data Security

All analytics and local storage operations are designed to minimize data collection.
No sensitive personal data (such as payment info or login details) is processed or stored by us.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Clear your localStorage data through your browser settings
  • Use privacy-focused browser extensions or settings to block analytics
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions
